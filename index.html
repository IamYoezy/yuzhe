I am Yuzhe Zhang (张语哲), an Early Career Research Fellow at Data61 of CSIRO, in Sydney, Australia. Before that, I did my Ph.D. of AI at the Bernoulli institute of the University of Groningen (2018 Nov. - 2023 Jan.), the Netherlands, supervised by Prof. Dr. Davide Grossi and Prof. Dr. Rineke Verbrugge. Further before, I did my master of AI at Kyushu University (2016-2018), Japan, supervised by Prof. Makoto Yokoo

My current research interests mainly include causal inference, computational social choice, algorithmic game theory, and application of voting in blockchain. Broadly speaking, I am interested in analysing the uncertainty aspect of algorithms.
