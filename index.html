Yuzhe Zhang yoezy.zhang@rug.nl University of Groningen

I am Yuzhe Zhang (张语哲), a Ph.D. candidate of Artificial Intelligence, in the Bernoulli institute at the University of Groningen (from 2018), the Netherlands, and supervised by Prof. Dr. Davide Grossi and Prof. Dr. Rineke Verbrugge. Before coming to Groningen, I did my master of AI at Kyushu University (2016-2018), Japan, and was supervised by Prof. Makoto Yokoo. Further before, I did my Bachelor of Mathematics and Economics (double degrees) at the Southwestern University of Finance and Economics (2011-2015), China.

My current research is mainly in the fields of computational social choice, algorithmic game theory, mechanism design and multi-agent systems. I am interested in analysing the interaction among intelligent agents, especially the theoretical aspect.
